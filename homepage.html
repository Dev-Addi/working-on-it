About Us

At VBA, we pride ourselves in being able to provide our clients with simple, efficient and professional service. We assist in the Renewal of Vehicle Licences, Vehicle Registration, Vehicle Deregistration, Change of Ownership of a Vehicle, and more. We are aware of how difficult it is to find the time of day in order to accomplish these tasks, and so we hope to cut out the hassle for our clients completely. We are committed to getting the job done at an affordable price and we do every job with a smile. With us you will not only save time, but will receive a yearly renewal notice once loaded into our database and, with our knowledge and experience, will find all the information you need under one roof.
